# Privacy policy
## About the purpose of using personal information
- Personal information such as name and e-mail address when making inquiries will be used only for necessary communications and will not be used for purposes other than these purposes.
- When sending an inquiry email from the app, add the app name, app version, OS version, etc. to the title. This is to facilitate the response to your inquiry. It can be deleted and sent by the user.

## Disclosure of personal information to a third party
The acquired personal information will be managed appropriately and will not be disclosed to third parties except in the following cases.
- When the consent of the person is obtained
- When disclosure is required by law
